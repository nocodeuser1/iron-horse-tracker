## Usage Guidelines

### Navigation Bar
- Background: `--burgundy-700` (#A43850)
- Logo: White version (`IH White Logo.png`)
- Links: White text with gold hover

### Buttons
- Primary: `--burgundy-700` background, white text
- Secondary: `--gold-500` background, white text
- Outline: `--burgundy-700` border with transparent background

### Status Colors
- Completed: `--gold-500` (positive, matches brand)
- Pending: `--neutral-400`
- Overdue: Red (#DC2626)

### Charts
- Use burgundy and gold as primary chart colors
- Complement with neutral grays

### Cards & Backgrounds
- Page background: `--neutral-50`
- Card background: White
- Card borders: `--neutral-200`
- Hover: `--burgundy-50` (very light burgundy tint)

## Typography
- Headings: Bold, `--burgundy-900` or `--burgundy-800`
- Body: `--neutral-700`
- Muted: `--neutral-500`
